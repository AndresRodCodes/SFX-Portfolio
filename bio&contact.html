My name is Fernanda Vazquez and I was born and raised in the Los Angeles area. I'm 26 years old and have found a deep passion for special effects make up and body painting. Halloween and Instagram played the biggest roles in allowing me to find my passion. Seeing other artists posting creepy make up looks inspired me to try it myself. What started off as just a hobby years ago has now turned into something I wish to make a profession out of. I've taken two make up courses so far and hope to take more in the future because I am a quick and eager learner and there is still so much I wish to learn.
